### FileVault Full Disk Encryption — personal MacBook and iMac

**Why:**
FileVault encrypts the entire startup disk for security.

**How:**
1. Identified that neither devices had FileVault enabled: System Settings > Network > Firewall
2. Enabled FileVault
3. Used the login passwords as the decryption keys and stored offline

### Windows Device Encryption - personal Lenovo laptop

**Why:**
Device Encryption encrypts the entire drive for security.

**How:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Device Encryption > Enabled
2. Found 48-digit BitLocker recovery key from Microsoft account online and stored offline
